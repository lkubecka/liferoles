Tips how to use this application

Role-based planing system

In this time management system it is important to determine which roles do you play in your life eg. father, husband, manager, lector, sportsman. Each task should be assigned to one of your roles. With this system you have control about how much effort do you make in your single roles.


Week planing

You should make your plan for the whole week. Find your time for planing for example at Sunday evening and try to write down all tasks for the next week. With weekly planing you are able to satisfy all your roles.


Personal mission

Personal mission is here to motivate yourself. When you determined your roles, write down your personal mission. Which goals do you want achieve in your life? Which bad habits you want to reduce? Feel free to edit your personal mission latter to keep it actual. Try to follow your words.
